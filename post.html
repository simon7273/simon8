Options Trading

Learn the basics of trading options

What Are Options?

Options are financial contracts that give buyers the right (but not the obligation) to buy or sell an asset at a specific price before a specific date.

Why Trade Options?

  1. Leverage
    • Control larger positions with less capital
    • Higher profit potential
  2. Risk Management
    • Use puts to protect against market drops
    • Limit losses with defined-risk strategies
  3. Income Generation
    • Sell covered calls to collect premium
    • Earn from sideways markets
